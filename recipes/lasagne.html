Lasagne

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish consisting of layers of pasta sheets, minced meat sauce and cheese sauce. At its best, lasagna is succulently moist and rich.

Ingredients

Minced meat sauce

  • 400g ground beef, fat 10%
  • 3 (300g) onions
  • 4 to 5 clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp Valio Oivariini liquid
  • 2 tsp (400g) tomato paste
  • 1 dl water
  • 2 meat and root stock cube
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper

Cheese sauce

  • 3 tbsp Valio Oivariini liquid
  • 3 tbsp wheat flour
  • 6dl Valio Kiehu milk drink
  • 1 bag (150g) Valio Emmental grated
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground white pepper

For lubrication

  • 1-2 tbsp Valio Oivariini liquid

Topping

  • 1dl Valio grated pizza cheese

Steps

Minced meat sauce

  1. Fry the minced meat and chopped onions and garlic in a pan in fat.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes. Rinse both cans with Â½ dl of water and add to the minced meat. Add stock cubes, sugar and pepper. Let it boil on low heat for a few minutes.

Cheese sauce

  1. Heat the fat in a pot and boil the wheat flour in it.
  2. Add the milk gradually and mix until smooth. Heat while stirring and add grated cheese. Season the sauce.

Making the lasagne

  1. Spread the minced meat sauce first and then the cheese sauce on the bottom of the greased lasagna pan. Place 3 lasagna sheets on top. Put minced meat sauce and cheese sauce on top again. Prepare the following layers in the same way.
  2. Finally, press the lasagna lightly so that the sauces are distributed evenly. Sprinkle grated cheese on top.
  3. Cook at 175 degrees for approx. 1 hour. Let rest for 15 to 20 minutes before serving.
